Spicy Pork

Ingredients

  • Pork (Shoulder/Butt is nice) (should have fat content)
  • Gochujang (Korean Red Pepper Paste)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Rice wine (optional)
  • Onion (optional)
  • Garlic (optional; powder is fine)
  • Starch (optional)
  • Seasame oil (optional)
  • Cooking oil

Cookingware

  • One pan
  • One container to mix
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Spatula / similar thing for flipping

Steps

  1. Start by cutting the pork into thin slices (not too large) and making sure the pork is semi-frozen will help.
  2. Cut the onions into semi circles; and peel the garlic then dice.
  3. Aggregate the cut ingredients together into a container. Add all other ingredients into the bowl (starch and later). Mix.
  4. If you have starch, that will be ideal (helps the sauce get nice and thiccccc instead of runny). Gradually add starch to the bowl and mix.
  5. You can either leave the bowl in the fridge to cook for another day/time (the meat will marinate better) or start cooking immediately.
  6. When cooking, heat the oil in pan first (can opt not to add oil since the fat on the pork will render), and try not to keep on flipping the meat over (you want some nice crusts on your slices of meat).
