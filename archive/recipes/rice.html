Rice

The Tatung brand cooker/steamer in our apartment works differently, so here's the steps for you useless chefs.

Steps

  1. Rinse your rice and try to drain all the water.
  2. Ratios: 1 cup of rice to 1 cup and 1 segment (measurement on the cup) of water to 1 1/6 segments of water in the cooker (not in the container but like in the cooker).
  3. Flip the switch.
  4. Wait 5-7 minutes after the switch pops for the rice to finish cooking; don't open the lid at all.
  5. Eat.
