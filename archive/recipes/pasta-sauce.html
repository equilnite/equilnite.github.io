Tomato-Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients

  • Alfredo sauce
  • Tomato sauce
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Vegetables (optional) (spinach or brocolli is good)
  • Meat (optional) (bacon/chicken)
  • Parmesan (optional)

Cookingware

  • One pan/pot (non-stick is better)
  • Ladel / similar thing for stirring or serving

Steps

  1. Add sauces to pan to heat (Ratio: 2 alfredo to 1 tomato). Use milk as a medium to clean out the jars (add then shake); add the milk to the mixture. Add butter (1 quarter to half a stick). Add as much parmesan as you want.
  2. If you have meat or vegetables, you can add them when the mixture is almost completely homogenized, or cook them separately on a pan / boiling water.
