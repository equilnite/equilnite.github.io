7 Steps to Get Into UC Berkeley

  1. Go to a very bad high school with only a little funding
  2. Take Precalc your freshman year and get 5's on AP Calc AB & BC and Stats (and sleep through all your classes).
  3. Join clubs that you don't even attend
  4. Medal in Science Olympiad Competitions
  5. Get a B on your UC Essays in class but submit them without fixing them
  6. Watch your friends get rejected from the UC's but feel sad for them because some of them tried really heard
  7. Get accepted into UCB
